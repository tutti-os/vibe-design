# ByteDance Corporate Reference

> Category: Global technology company

A clean global technology-company system inspired by ByteDance public pages. Use it for mission-led corporate pages, culture/value presentations, leadership and investor pages, company history timelines, careers, press, and global contact surfaces.

## Sources

- https://www.bytedance.com/en/

Use white and soft gray as the base. Blue is the primary corporate accent. Red and cyan can be used sparingly for creative-product energy, culture highlights, carousel controls, or milestone markers. Avoid heavy gradients and over-branded social-media styling.

## Typography

Use a global sans stack with crisp hierarchy and plain language. Large mission statements should be direct and optimistic. Culture cards and history entries need strong titles with concise supporting copy.

Recommended scale:

- Mission hero: 56-88px desktop, 40-54px mobile, 0.96-1.08 line-height.
- Section title: 32-48px, 1.08-1.18 line-height.
- Culture card title: 22-30px, 1.16-1.28 line-height.
- Body: 15-18px, 1.55-1.75 line-height.
- Navigation: 14-16px.
- Meta/year/contact labels: 12-14px.

## Layout

Use a minimal top navigation with About, Press, and Join Us style entry points. The page should open with a mission-first hero and supporting media grid or carousel. Subsequent sections should use clear blocks: culture values carousel, board/leadership grid, investor strip, company history narrative, milestone timeline, code of conduct callout, and contact footer.

Information density should be moderate. Cards can be large and breathable, but timelines and contact rows should be compact and easy to scan.

## Components

- Mission hero: company name, mission label, large mission statement, media carousel.
- Culture carousel: value title, icon/media slot, concise principle list, previous/next controls.
- Leadership grid: portrait slot, name, role, consistent card sizing.
- Investor strip: logo placeholders or text chips, alphabetical note.
- History block: long-form company story with wide readable measure.
- Milestone timeline: year/month marker, title, optional media slot, progress dots.
- Code of conduct callout: document CTA, ethics/integrity summary.
- Contact footer: media inquiries, careers, advertising, language switch, legal links.

## Interaction

Carousel controls should be visible and calm. Timeline navigation can use dots or vertical markers. Hover states should use border emphasis, subtle blue fill, or small translate. Language switching and footer links should remain understated and accessible.

## Boundaries

This package describes corporate structure and visual behavior only. Do not copy ByteDance logos, official photos, investor logos, culture illustrations, exact value text, or page copy. Generate original global technology-company content while preserving the mission-led structure, culture carousel rhythm, timeline storytelling, and direct contact taxonomy of the reference page.
