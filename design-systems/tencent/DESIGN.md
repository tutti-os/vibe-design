# Tencent Corporate Reference

> Category: Corporate technology

A Chinese corporate technology portal system inspired by Tencent public pages. Use it for large technology-company websites, corporate newsrooms, business architecture pages, ESG portals, investor relations, employee/careers pages, and public-trust communications.

## Sources

- https://www.tencent.com/

The system should feel corporate, trustworthy, and civic-minded. Use blue as the primary trust and navigation accent. Use green for ESG and public-good content, gold sparingly for investor or financial signals, and dark surfaces mainly for footer or media overlays.

## Typography

Use a Chinese-first enterprise sans stack. Headings should be clear and steady, with moderate weight and generous line-height. Avoid playful display typography. Dates, categories, and regulatory links should be compact and highly scannable.

Recommended scale:

- Hero headline: 44-68px desktop, 34-46px mobile, 1.05-1.15 line-height.
- Section title: 28-42px, 1.12-1.24 line-height.
- News title: 18-24px, 1.25-1.4 line-height.
- Body: 15-18px, 1.6-1.8 line-height.
- Navigation: 14-16px.
- Meta/date/legal: 12-14px.

## Layout

Use a large corporate header with multi-level navigation for company profile, business, employees, ESG, investors, and media. The homepage should support a top announcement/news band, latest-dynamics feed, pillar cards, business taxonomy grids, ESG/social-responsibility sections, and a dense footer.

Information architecture matters more than decoration. Use consistent sections, clear card groupings, dates, categories, and CTA links. Business pillars should express the connecting theme: users and life, enterprises and technology, present and future.

## Components

- Corporate header: wordmark text area, language switch, mega-navigation groups.
- Announcement carousel: compact headline list with date and active indicator.
- Latest-news grid: date, category, title, summary, and read-more affordance.
- Business pillar card: image/media slot, title, short mission sentence, category link.
- Business taxonomy tile: icon/media slot, business name, explanatory paragraph.
- ESG feature card: public-good story, environmental/social/governance tag, subdued green accent.
- Investor report row: quarter, report title, file/action links, financial metadata.
- Corporate footer: social links, recruitment, contact, legal, filings, copyright.
- Cookie banner: bilingual preference copy with accept/reject actions.

## Interaction

Navigation hover states should reveal grouped menus cleanly. News cards should underline titles or raise border contrast. Language switches and cookie preferences must be accessible and low-friction. Business/ESG cards can use subtle image zoom or blue overlay, but motion should stay restrained.

## Boundaries

This package describes corporate information architecture and visual behavior only. Do not copy Tencent logos, official photos, icons, article copy, regulatory wording, or exact page layouts. Generate original enterprise content while preserving the public-company structure, blue trust accent, Chinese corporate rhythm, and dense footer taxonomy of the reference page.
