# Alibaba Marketplace Reference

> Category: B2B marketplace

A high-density B2B sourcing marketplace system inspired by Alibaba.com public pages. Use it for global wholesale marketplaces, sourcing agents, RFQ flows, supplier catalogs, product discovery, category browsing, and buyer-protection surfaces.

## Sources

- https://www.alibaba.com/

Orange is the core commerce and CTA accent. Use green for buyer protection, verified supplier, and order assurance states. Blue is secondary for logistics, help, and information links. Keep backgrounds clean so product/category density remains readable.

## Typography

Use a practical marketplace sans stack. Text should be compact and scannable, with strong search labels, concise category names, and clear price/supplier hierarchy.

Recommended scale:

- Marketplace hero: 44-68px desktop, 34-46px mobile, 1.02-1.14 line-height.
- Search prompt: 18-24px.
- Section title: 24-36px.
- Product title: 14-18px, 1.25-1.4 line-height.
- Body: 14-16px, 1.45-1.65 line-height.
- Metadata, MOQ, shipping, protection: 12-14px.

## Layout

Use a commerce header with category navigation, buyer/seller/help links, and app/extension affordances. The primary interaction is a large search or AI sourcing prompt. Follow it with RFQ entry points, category grids, frequently searched chips, product/supplier cards, and trust/protection modules.

Marketplace pages should favor dense but organized grids. Product cards need stable image ratios, price/MOQ rows, supplier trust badges, rating or response indicators, and quick inquiry actions.

## Components

- Marketplace header: all categories, featured selections, order protection, buyer central, help center, sell CTA.
- AI sourcing hero: prompt/search bar, product/manufacturer tabs, agent explanation, primary source button.
- RFQ card: requirements prompt, quote count cue, quick post action.
- Category grid: icon or image slot, short category label, responsive multi-column layout.
- Product card: image slot, title, price range, MOQ, supplier badge, inquiry button.
- Supplier card: company name, verified/trade assurance badge, response metric, country/region.
- Hot search chips: compact scrollable chips for popular products.
- Buyer protection strip: order assurance, payment security, logistics, refund or dispute hints.

## Interaction

Search and RFQ should be visually dominant and fast to use. Category and product cards should brighten border or reveal quick actions on hover. Trust badges must stay readable at small sizes. AI agent states should show suggestion chips, sourcing progress, and matched supplier counts.

## Boundaries

This package describes marketplace structure and sourcing behavior only. Do not copy Alibaba logos, product photos, supplier names, exact category artwork, or page copy. Generate original B2B marketplace content while preserving the orange commerce accent, dense sourcing workflow, category breadth, and buyer-protection cues of the reference page.
